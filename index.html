Welcome to High School Riunion! This is a point-and-click adventure where you have to talk with all the Ris to see if they're still the same zany little dudes from their high school days. Talk with every Ri in order to unlock the special birthday door and view the special birthday ending!

This is made for @Rioisok's 20th birthday. Happy birthday dude! <3

Controls:
Click with the left mouse button to travel into new rooms (by clicking doors) and talk with a Ri (by clicking a Ri). Have fun!
